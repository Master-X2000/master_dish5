Chocolate Milk

Little Info About The Food

Chocolate milk is another drink that can be had post-workout. The drink contains carbohydrates and protein required for muscle recovery. The water content will also replace the fluids lost as sweat and milk and will provide calcium that will help the body to recover faster.
